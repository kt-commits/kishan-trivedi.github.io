About

Hi! My name is Kishan Trivedi. I am a graduate student at the Viterbi School Of Engineering, USC pursuing my Masters in Computer Science. I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science from the University of Mumbai, India.

I have significant experience in Enterprise Software development, Web development, Data Analysis, DevOps, and Testing. My main interests are in the domains of distributed systems, software engineering, and machine learning.

At Edelweiss financial services, I primarily worked as a Java Developer on a real-time post-trade risk analysis software for the Global Markets Trading Desk. The application achieved low latency, high throughput, and high scalability by using Kafka + Esper CEP as the main data-flow medium. During my tenure, I gained relevant experience in designing enterprise-level software by implementing SOLID design principles. Apart from the technical aspect, I also gained considerable knowledge about the financial world that mainly included derivative pricing and risk modeling for trading.

My hobbies include problem-solving, playing soccer (Fc Barcelona fan!), reading, trekking, and playing the guitar.
